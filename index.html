How to sell on OpenSVM P2P Exchange

1. Connect your wallet

Start by connecting your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.) to the OpenSVM P2P Exchange. This allows you to securely sell your crypto across any SVM network.

2. Choose your network and cryptocurrency

Select which SVM network you want to use (Solana, Sonic, Eclipse, svmBNB, or s00n) and which cryptocurrency you want to sell. Each network has different confirmation times and gas fees.

3. Find the best offer

Browse through available buyer offers, comparing rates, payment methods, and verification requirements. Choose the offer that best suits your needs.

4. Complete the secure trade

Once you select an offer, your crypto will be held in a secure escrow smart contract on the selected SVM network. After the buyer sends payment, you can release the crypto to complete the trade.
